This is LTL2Rabin, a tool that generates a generalized deterministic rabin automaton from an LTL formula. It is released under the Apache 2.0 license.

The main class is {@link ltl2rabin.Main}. The class that contains the core functionality, the computation of the GDRA, is the {@link ltl2rabin.GDRAFactory} class.

We distinguish between {@link ltl2rabin.LTL.Formula}, which has limited functionality, and {@link ltl2rabin.LTL.PropEquivalenceClass}, which defines the behaviour for propositionally equivalent sets of formulas.

This picture gives an overview over the construction:

The input is handled by {@link ltl2rabin.Main}. The parsing is handled by an {@link ltl2rabin.LTL.LTLFactory}. A {@link ltl2rabin.MojmirAutomaton} is constructed by a {@link ltl2rabin.MojmirAutomatonFactory}. A {@link ltl2rabin.SubAutomaton} is constructed by a {@link ltl2rabin.SubAutomatonFromMojmirFactory} or a {@link ltl2rabin.SubAutomatonFromFormulaFactory}. The Master Transition System, the product and the acceptance conditions are computed by a {@link ltl2rabin.GDRAFactory}.
